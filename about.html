About

Rainbow Land Activity Centre is committed to providing children with quality early childhood care and education. We understand the importance of giving children the best start in life and strive to create a safe, supportive and stimulating environment that encourages children to learn, play and grow.

Our team of kindergarten teachers, helpers and activity managers ensure best experience for every child.

Learning with Fun

Preschool education is important for children aged 2-5 to learn social, emotional and cognitive skills. Children learn to strengthen their social development through games, drawing and music, gain a sense of self, play with peers, and build self-confidence. Quality preschool helps children to find answers through explorations, experimentations, and conversations, providing valuable tools for school readiness. Parents can have peace of mind knowing that their children are getting a head start in life.

  • ● Enhanced Motor Skills
  • ● Ability to Learn Languages

Day Care Centre

Our Daycare Centre is an excellent way for parents to give their children a safe, nurturing, and stimulating learning environment to grow. Children can participate in activities at a daycare centre that helps them develop their interpersonal skills and cognitive abilities. The daycare centre provides parents with their much-needed break while providing kids with a safe space to develop essential life skills.


Not only does this help them develop essential skills for success later in life, but it also offers parents a much-needed break.
