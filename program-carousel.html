About

the kindergarten care program at Rainbow Land Activity Centre

Early childhood development is critically important. At Rainbow Land Activity Centre, our experienced teachers and staff help facilitate this learning period, which separates us from traditional childcare facilities..

With our New Beginnings program, your baby will be paired with a primary teacher who will provide personalised attention and nurture them through every milestone—within a caring and cohesive community.

If you’re ready for more info, we’d love for you to come visit us.

Transform the future with early learning

Our kindergarten program includes:

  • Children are encouraged to participate in exciting and interactive activities that allow them to explore and learn through fun and interesting experiences.
  • Interesting Learning Activities: Children are offered interesting learning activities, stimulating discussions and team-building exercises that challenge their creativity and mental growth.
  • Curriculum that supports creative expression, literacy, music, and more

Kindergarten Program

We’ve designed our program to give you complete confidence in every aspect of your child’s experience, from health and safety to the ready-for-school curriculum, to the full support of experienced teachers.

But, just as important, our program is based on what research says best prepares children for kindergarten... and all the significant steps ahead.

Learning at Rainbow Land Kindergarten and Activity Centre in Virar.

Rainbow Land Kindergarten in Virar West is established to provide quality kindergarten education. We are proud to be one of the best Montessori kindergartens in the area, with experienced teachers, caring staff and helpers committed to providing our students with the best care and education. We offer a wide range of services, including daycare, kindergarten classes, playgroups, art and music classes, and more.

  • ✅ Everyday Mathematics: Integrating numbers into real-life routines
  • ✅ Daily Pre-Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking: Stories and literature to promote the understanding of the relationship between speech and print
  • ✅ Writing Center: Access to a wide variety of writing tools, paper, and other materials to inspire an interest in writing, creating books, and more

Enrol for Admissions Today

Admission to Rainbow Land Kindergarten has started for the academic year 2023–2024.

While most families complete registration and admissions by the end of February, we offer admissions on a First come, first-serve basis.

We have limited seats for the Academic year 2023 – 2024 and thus request parents to register before the end of February and confirm their admissions in advance.

Admissions are confirmed post-payment of fees only.
